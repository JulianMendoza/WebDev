Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

By Jean-August-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière was painted by the French Neoclassical artist Jean August Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family in the artist painted that year

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimension: 100 x 70 cm
Home: Musèe du Louve, Paris
